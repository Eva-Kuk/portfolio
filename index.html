About me

Hello! My name is Eva and I am a Junior Front End Web Developer based in Dublin. I obtained a certificate from Code Institute as a Full Stack Web Developer and also have a background in technical engineering, and in teaching computer science. In my role as a developer, I specialize in creating scalable web applications and working across the full stack of development. I am particularly passionate about front-end development and UX design. I find great joy in combining the worlds of logic and creative design to craft visually appealing, accessible, and user-friendly websites and applications. Outside of my professional pursuits, I have a variety of hobbies and interests. I enjoy practicing yoga, riding motorbikes, exploring nature through hiking, and embarking on exciting adventures while traveling. These activities provide me with balance and inspiration in my life.
